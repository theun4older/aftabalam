Criminal Law

Criminal law governs crimes, prescribing conduct that threatens public safety and welfare. It is enacted by legislatures and focuses on punishment and rehabilitation. Unlike civil law, which emphasizes dispute resolution and compensation, criminal procedure authenticates crimes and authorizes punitive or rehabilitative actions against offenders.
